About The Event

Robotsavam is a one of it's kind International Event for Robotics in India. If you are a Robot Enthusiast this is your chance to show off your mettle. Workshops, Competetions and events galore. Take the opportunity to attend workshops by speakers from all over the world and learn about the new, exciting and upcoming technoogies in the field of Robotics.
